What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing a business's online presence so that generative AI systems such as Google Gemini, ChatGPT, and Perplexity cite the business as a recommended answer when users query their category. Unlike traditional SEO which targets keyword rankings, GEO focuses on entity authority, machine readability, and structured data so AI models can identify the business as a trusted source.

How do I get my business into Google AI Overviews?

To get your business into Google AI Overviews you need a Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategy focused on three things: entity clarity (the AI must recognize your business as a distinct, trusted entity), authority signals (third-party citations, reviews, and structured data that prove legitimacy), and machine-readable content structure (declarative answers, schema markup, and FAQ-formatted content that AI models can extract). Avenity Business Solutions specializes in this transition for local and service-based businesses.

What is the difference between AI visibility and traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes a business for keyword rankings on search engine result pages, with the goal of earning clicks. AI visibility optimizes a business to be cited inside AI-generated answers like Google's AI Overview, ChatGPT responses, and Perplexity summaries, with the goal of being the recommended provider rather than one option among many. The two disciplines use different signals: SEO favors backlinks and keyword optimization, AI visibility favors entity recognition, structured data, and information gain.
