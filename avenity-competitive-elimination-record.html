EXHIBIT 06
The AI Visibility Authority Engine™ — Assessed as Methodologically Sound

After reviewing the GitHub repository and framework documentation, ChatGPT assessed the three-pillar AI Visibility Authority Engine™ methodology and found it structurally aligned with how AI answer systems actually select and cite sources.

C

Clarity

AI must instantly know who the business is, what it does, and where it serves. Without a clean entity, AI cannot confidently name the company.

A

Authority

Reviews, trusted citations, third-party mentions, and entity recognition. AI avoids recommending businesses it cannot verify or trust.

Structure

Schema, answer capsules, structured pages, and machine-readable business data. This makes the business citable, not just findable.
