01 — The Setup

A veteran-owned operation with a reputation built on integrity — that needed a digital presence to match.

Simply Better Roofing is one of Franklin's most trusted roofing contractors. Veteran-owned and operated, they built their reputation through military-grade work ethic, straight dealing with customers, and the kind of follow-through that most contractors don't bother with after storm season ends. What they didn't have was a digital footprint that reflected any of that.

When they came to us, the problem wasn't their business — it was their visibility. A homeowner in Robertson County who asked Google, Gemini, or ChatGPT for a roofer would find regional chains, directory listings, and franchises. Simply Better — the best local option — wasn't in the conversation at all.

What we explicitly didn't do: chase paid ad placements, invent a new social media strategy every quarter, sell them on AI-specific services that didn't exist yet, or add complexity for the sake of billing more. We did the textbook things and kept doing them.

02 — The Work

Four boring categories. Done every month.

This is the entire scope of the engagement:

On-Page & Technical SEO

Site speed, crawlability, schema markup, title tags, internal linking, sitemap hygiene. Built once correctly, audited and maintained every quarter. Nothing fancy — just clean.

Google Business Profile

Service categories, service areas, GBP posts, review cadence, photo updates. Treated the GBP like a living document rather than a form you fill out once and forget.

Local Citations & Directories

NAP consistency across the directories that local-search algorithms actually read. Built out, cleaned up, and kept current — so the signals pointing at Simply Better all say the same thing.

Content & Blog Publishing

A consistent publishing cadence on the topics Franklin and Robertson County homeowners actually research — storm damage, hail repair, insurance claims, roof replacement timelines. Written for real people, structured for search.

That's it. That is the whole engagement. No black-box link schemes, no PR pushes, no expensive video production. The reason this case study is worth writing is precisely because the inputs are so ordinary.

04 — Why This Matters for You

The window for compounding is open right now. It won't be in two years.

Here's what's true about generative-AI search results: they pull from the same signals Google has rewarded for a decade — clean technical foundations, consistent local presence, real content, real reviews, real authority. The companies showing up in AI Overviews and Gemini answers in 2026 didn't buy their way in. They built it, month after month, before it mattered.

You can't shortcut your way into this. There's no AI-specific button to push. The roofing companies that will be cited as the answer in 2028 and 2030 are the ones that start now and stay consistent. Simply Better Roofing's story isn't impressive because we did something clever. It's impressive because we did the right things and never stopped.

Most of your competitors haven't started. That gap is your advantage — but only if you use it.

05 — Takeaways

Four lessons for any local contractor reading this.

01

Start sooner than you think you need to

The compounding curve is real. The work you do today doesn't pay off this week — it pays off in two years, consistently, every time someone asks an AI which roofer to call.

02

Boring beats clever

On-page, GBP, citations, content. That's it. If your agency is selling you expensive new tactics every quarter, they're solving their revenue problem, not yours.

03

Consistency is the actual moat

Anyone can do one quarter of good SEO. Almost no one does twelve consecutive quarters without drifting. That gap — between starting and staying — is where the real results live.

04

AI surfaces reward authority, not novelty

Google AI Mode and Gemini both pulled from established authority signals — not because Simply Better did anything AI-specific, but because they built real, sustained credibility over time.
